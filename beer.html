Local Breweries

Odell Brewing Company

Knock off one of two major Fort Collins breweries with a trip to Odell. Spend part of your day sampling some of their award winning beer: Easy Street Wheat, 90 Shilling, Irish Red, Friek.

Odell offers a spacious outdoor patio for seating, yard games, and a lawn area for your pets to roam. Be sure to check out their events page

What are you waiting for? Book your next tour here

New Belgium

A trip to New Belgium is a must. New Belgium is now the 4th largest craft brewery in the United States and has 13 year round beer available, ranging from IPA, Ale, Lager, Pilsner.

New Belgium offers a very unique and thorough tour of their brewing process. Tours fill up fast and reservations are needed often 2 months in advance. Click here to make a reservation

Perfect for the day trip on the weekend, to lounge on their patio and try any of the various food truck or other fun events

Oskar Blues

Oskar Blues is the first brewery to release a canned craft beer in the United States. Many of its well known beers include: Dale's Pale Ale, Old Chub, Mama's Little Yella Pils - all their beer is mainly pale ale or pilsner.

Located in Longmont you can either visit their tap room Tasty Weasel or Oskar Blues Restaurant

Want some other food options? Not a problem, Oskbar Blues owns and operates Chuburger Cyclhops and Hotbox all in the Rino or Longmont area.

Great Divide

Looking to stay in Denver? This is the place for you. 2 Taprooms are a short walk from Coors Field or just 1 mile further north in the Rino disctrict. Be sure to explore the many other breweries in the Rino area.

Great Divide's beer selection includes: Imperial Stout, Pale Ale, Nitro, Scotch Ale, Farmhouse Ale, Saison - with these fun branding names:

  • Tank Farm
  • Yeti Clan

Food trucks are plentiful various days of the week.
